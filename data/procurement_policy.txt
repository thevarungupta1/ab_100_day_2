Contoso Manufacturing Supplier Onboarding Policy

All new suppliers must complete due diligence before purchase orders are issued.
Suppliers with annual expected spend above USD 250,000 require enhanced verification.
Suppliers operating in high-risk jurisdictions must provide additional ownership disclosure.
Procurement must maintain audit logs for every onboarding decision for seven years.
Auto-approval is allowed only when risk score is below enterprise threshold and no sanctions match exists.
Rejected suppliers may reapply after remediation evidence is submitted and reviewed by Compliance.
