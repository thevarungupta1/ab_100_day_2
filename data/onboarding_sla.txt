Supplier Onboarding SLA and Workflow Standards

Standard onboarding target: 5 business days for low-risk suppliers.
High-risk onboarding target: 10 business days including human review.
Mandatory workflow stages: Intake, Due Diligence, Policy Check, Risk Scoring, Approval, ERP Registration.
Workflow automation must emit trace events for each stage transition.
If human review is required, assign to Regional Procurement Manager queue.
ERP registration is blocked until approval status is Approved.
