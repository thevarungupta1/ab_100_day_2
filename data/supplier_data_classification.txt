Supplier Data Classification Standards

Supplier tax IDs, bank details, and contract rates are classified as Confidential.
Public marketing information may be stored in general collaboration tools.
Confidential supplier data must remain in approved systems: SAP Ariba, Azure SQL, and SharePoint Secure Library.
Data residency: EU supplier records must remain in EU Azure regions.
Retention: inactive supplier records must be archived after 24 months and deleted after legal hold release.
